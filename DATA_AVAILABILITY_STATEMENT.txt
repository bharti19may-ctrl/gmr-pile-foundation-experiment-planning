The derived data, numerical results and analysis code supporting this study are openly available in the GitHub repository at https://github.com/bharti19may-ctrl/gmr-pile-foundation-experiment-planning. The original processed strong-motion records are publicly available from the Center for Engineering Strong Motion Data (CESMD), operated by the USGS and California Geological Survey, at https://www.strongmotioncenter.org/, using the event and station identifiers reported in Table 1 and Supplementary Table S1. The original CESMD waveforms are not redistributed. No empirical GMR measurement dataset was generated or analysed in this study.
